Surf lessons in Hossegor

Learn to surf in one of the most renowned surf towns in Europe. Small groups of 6 maximum, board and wetsuit included, with certified coaches.

Book a lessonSee rates

Hossegor is the heart of surfing in the Landes, a reference for beach breaks in Europe. Coco Surf School is a mobile school between Hossegor and Seignosse: we choose the best spot for your level and the conditions of the day.

Why learn in Hossegor?

  • Atlantic beach breaks suited to every level, from white water for beginners to punchier peaks.
  • A town built around surfing, ideal for a stay with your feet in the water.
  • Wide sandy beaches a stone’s throw from the ocean.

Lessons for every level

Never surfed, or want to progress? Every session adapts to you. Lessons last 1h30, board and wetsuit included, in groups of 6 maximum (8 on request).

Good to know

The best spot — Hossegor or Seignosse — is chosen based on the tide, swell and wind. In summer the school is based at Seignosse, Les Bourdaines.
