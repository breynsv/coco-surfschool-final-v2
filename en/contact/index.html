FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Where do the surf lessons take place?+

Coco Surf School is a mobile school between Hossegor and Seignosse: the best spot is chosen for your level and the conditions. In summer the school is based at Seignosse, Les Bourdaines.

How many people are there per lesson?+

Quality over quantity: maximum 6 people per instructor (8 on request), for more coaching and faster progression.

Is the equipment included?+

Yes, board and wetsuit are included in every lesson. Each lesson lasts 1h30.

How do I book and pay?+

A 30% deposit is requested on booking. Payment is by bank transfer or cash.

Do I need to know how to surf already?+

No. Annelies welcomes beginners as well as surfers who want to progress.
