Your coach

Your coach

“Surfing changed my life: a new world opened up! Sports and coaching are my life.”

Annelies is here to introduce you to surfing or help you progress! A fan of surfing and many other sports, born in Belgium where she trained at a high level in trampoline, she discovered surfing at 16 on a family holiday in Soustons.

After a Master in Physical Education she followed her dream and went surfing, snowboarding and working around the world: Australia, New Zealand, USA, Indonesia, Sri Lanka, Fiji, Morocco… “After my life as a competitor, it was so logical to become a sports, surf and snowboard teacher.”

After 10 years of travelling, the ocean called her back and she settled in Seignosse. “I had the honour of working with the Hossegor Surf Club; today I’m delighted to welcome you to my own school. Coco Surf School focuses on quality, not quantity: small-group lessons of maximum 6 people.”

— Annelies
